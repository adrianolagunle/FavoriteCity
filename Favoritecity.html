Adrian's Favorite City

My favorite city is Chicago, Illinois!

Around Town in Chicago

Chicago is where I was born and raised and most of my family lives there. Some of my favorite things to do around town in the city is to visit Navy Pier, visit Millennium Park, go to the beaches on Lake Michigan, look around the downtown area, and go to various sporting events. The city has a great sports fanbase. Chicago is known to be a cold weather city, but I am used to the cold weather. The city's skyline is spectacular especially viewed at night. The lights on the buildings make for a great photograph.

Facts About Chicago

  • The city was founded by a black man by the name of Jean Pointe Baptiste DuSable.
  • The current population of the city as of 2017 is 2.7 million.
  • Chicago has five major sports teams: Bears, Bulls, Blackhawks, Cubs, and White Sox.
  • On a clear day from the top of the city's tallest building, the Willis Tower, you can see up to 50 miles in four different states: Illinois, Indiana, Wisconsin, and Michigan.
  • Chicago is known for their deep dish style pizza!
  • Nicknames for the city include The Windy City and Second City.
  • Wrigley Field (home of the Cubs) is the second oldest park in Major League Baseball.
